# ARITHMETIC PROBLEMS - COMPREHENSIVE COLLECTION
# Basic arithmetic problems with solutions and explanations

# BASIC ADDITION
Problem: 5 + 3 = ?
Solution: 8
Explanation: Adding 5 and 3 gives 8. Start at 5 and count 3 more: 6, 7, 8.

Problem: 12 + 7 = ?
Solution: 19
Explanation: Adding 12 and 7 gives 19. 12 + 7 = 19.

Problem: 25 + 18 = ?
Solution: 43
Explanation: 25 + 18 = 43. Add the tens: 20 + 10 = 30. Add the ones: 5 + 8 = 13. 30 + 13 = 43.

Problem: 156 + 89 = ?
Solution: 245
Explanation: 156 + 89 = 245. Add hundreds: 100 + 0 = 100. Add tens: 50 + 80 = 130. Add ones: 6 + 9 = 15. 100 + 130 + 15 = 245.

Problem: 789 + 456 = ?
Solution: 1245
Explanation: 789 + 456 = 1245. Use column addition: 9+6=15 (write 5, carry 1), 8+5+1=14 (write 4, carry 1), 7+4+1=12. Result: 1245.

# BASIC SUBTRACTION
Problem: 8 - 3 = ?
Solution: 5
Explanation: Subtracting 3 from 8 gives 5. 8 - 3 = 5.

Problem: 15 - 7 = ?
Solution: 8
Explanation: 15 - 7 = 8. Start at 15 and count back 7: 14, 13, 12, 11, 10, 9, 8.

Problem: 42 - 19 = ?
Solution: 23
Explanation: 42 - 19 = 23. Subtract tens: 40 - 10 = 30. Subtract ones: 2 - 9 = -7. 30 - 7 = 23.

Problem: 156 - 78 = ?
Solution: 78
Explanation: 156 - 78 = 78. Use column subtraction: 6-8 needs borrowing, 16-8=8, 4-7 needs borrowing, 14-7=7, 0-0=0. Result: 78.

Problem: 500 - 234 = ?
Solution: 266
Explanation: 500 - 234 = 266. 500 - 200 = 300. 300 - 34 = 266.

# BASIC MULTIPLICATION
Problem: 4 × 3 = ?
Solution: 12
Explanation: 4 × 3 = 12. Four groups of three: 3 + 3 + 3 + 3 = 12.

Problem: 7 × 8 = ?
Solution: 56
Explanation: 7 × 8 = 56. Seven groups of eight: 8 + 8 + 8 + 8 + 8 + 8 + 8 = 56.

Problem: 12 × 5 = ?
Solution: 60
Explanation: 12 × 5 = 60. Twelve groups of five: 12 × 5 = (10 × 5) + (2 × 5) = 50 + 10 = 60.

Problem: 23 × 4 = ?
Solution: 92
Explanation: 23 × 4 = 92. 20 × 4 = 80. 3 × 4 = 12. 80 + 12 = 92.

Problem: 156 × 3 = ?
Solution: 468
Explanation: 156 × 3 = 468. 100 × 3 = 300. 50 × 3 = 150. 6 × 3 = 18. 300 + 150 + 18 = 468.

Problem: 45 × 23 = ?
Solution: 1035
Explanation: 45 × 23 = 1035. 45 × 20 = 900. 45 × 3 = 135. 900 + 135 = 1035.

# MIXED OPERATIONS
Problem: 5 + 3 × 2 = ?
Solution: 11
Explanation: 5 + 3 × 2 = 5 + 6 = 11. Follow order of operations: multiplication before addition.

Problem: (5 + 3) × 2 = ?
Solution: 16
Explanation: (5 + 3) × 2 = 8 × 2 = 16. Parentheses first, then multiplication.

Problem: 20 - 4 ÷ 2 = ?
Solution: 18
Explanation: 20 - 4 ÷ 2 = 20 - 2 = 18. Division before subtraction.

Problem: 15 + 7 - 3 = ?
Solution: 19
Explanation: 15 + 7 - 3 = 22 - 3 = 19. Addition and subtraction from left to right.

Problem: 6 × 4 ÷ 3 = ?
Solution: 8
Explanation: 6 × 4 ÷ 3 = 24 ÷ 3 = 8. Multiplication and division from left to right.

# WORD PROBLEMS
Problem: John has 5 apples and gets 3 more. How many apples does John have now?
Solution: 8 apples
Explanation: 5 + 3 = 8. John starts with 5 apples and gets 3 more, so he has 8 apples.

Problem: Sarah has 15 stickers and gives 7 to her friend. How many stickers does Sarah have left?
Solution: 8 stickers
Explanation: 15 - 7 = 8. Sarah starts with 15 stickers and gives away 7, so she has 8 left.

Problem: A classroom has 4 rows of desks with 6 desks in each row. How many desks are there in total?
Solution: 24 desks
Explanation: 4 × 6 = 24. There are 4 rows with 6 desks each, so 4 × 6 = 24 desks.

Problem: A pizza is cut into 8 equal slices. If 3 friends each eat 2 slices, how many slices are left?
Solution: 2 slices
Explanation: 3 friends × 2 slices each = 6 slices eaten. 8 - 6 = 2 slices left.

Problem: Tom has $50 and buys 3 books that cost $12 each. How much money does Tom have left?
Solution: $14
Explanation: 3 × $12 = $36 spent. $50 - $36 = $14 left.

# FRACTION PROBLEMS
Problem: 1/2 + 1/4 = ?
Solution: 3/4
Explanation: 1/2 + 1/4 = 2/4 + 1/4 = 3/4. Find common denominator (4) and add numerators.

Problem: 3/4 - 1/2 = ?
Solution: 1/4
Explanation: 3/4 - 1/2 = 3/4 - 2/4 = 1/4. Find common denominator (4) and subtract numerators.

Problem: 2/3 × 3/4 = ?
Solution: 1/2
Explanation: 2/3 × 3/4 = (2 × 3)/(3 × 4) = 6/12 = 1/2. Multiply numerators and denominators, then simplify.

Problem: 3/4 ÷ 1/2 = ?
Solution: 3/2 or 1 1/2
Explanation: 3/4 ÷ 1/2 = 3/4 × 2/1 = 6/4 = 3/2. Divide by multiplying by reciprocal.

# PRIME AND COMPOSITE
Problem: Is 17 prime or composite?
Solution: Prime
Explanation: 17 is prime because its only factors are 1 and 17.

Problem: Is 12 prime or composite?
Solution: Composite
Explanation: 12 is composite because it has factors other than 1 and itself: 2, 3, 4, 6.

# GREATEST COMMON FACTOR
Problem: Find GCF of 12 and 18.
Solution: 6
Explanation: Factors of 12: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12. Factors of 18: 1, 2, 3, 6, 9, 18. Greatest common factor is 6.

# LEAST COMMON MULTIPLE
Problem: Find LCM of 4 and 6.
Solution: 12
Explanation: Multiples of 4: 4, 8, 12, 16... Multiples of 6: 6, 12, 18... Least common multiple is 12.
